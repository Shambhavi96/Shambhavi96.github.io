IRIS

We are offering full suite of solutions to tackle multifaceted problems. Our first module is for tracking and understanding the demographics of visitors to the museum. The second module is a knowledge platform. The Iris platform is a forum for conservationists to interact within the community along with the Q&A module that can act as a companion app during museum visits.

VISITOR ENGAGEMENT

A companion application for an immersive in-museum experience.


Forum

A community coming together to solve problems is a lot more efficient than a single person trying to tackle a problem. Through Iris, all conservationists can discuss on a common platform and through the combined efforts of people around the world new and innovative techniques can be discovered and talked about here.

Read more

Q & A Generating/Answering system

Making the entire museum going experience more engaging for the visitor through a companion application. The visitor can scan the name tag of the exhibit and get further information about it. Using the wikipedia articles about the exhibit we generate potential questions and answers the user could ask about the exhibit using AI.
