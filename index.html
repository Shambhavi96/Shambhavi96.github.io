Q & A Generating/Answering system

Through this module we aim to increase user interaction with the art piece/exhibit. If a visitor would like to know more about a particular art piece they could scan the name tag of the exhibit and automatically we would give a possible set of questions that the visitor could ask generated from the available audio transcripts or the wikipedia articles. Through this way, we reduce the effort by the user. Apart from the generated questions, the user can also ask other questions. In addition to the questions, we also provide with relevant Wikipedia pages and video from YouTube.
